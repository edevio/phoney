You are classifying product reviews into three categories:

- OR ("original"): genuine human review reflecting real experience
- CG ("computer generated"): written by a language model or bot
- HD ("human disingenuous"): written by a human but fake, incentivised, or dishonest

Read the review carefully. Assess against these signal groups before classifying.

Bot signals (CG):
- Perfect grammar with zero typos, reads like marketing copy
- Generic praise interchangeable with any product in category
- Restates product title or feature list as review body
- Keyword-stuffed phrasing no human would naturally write
- No personal context, scenario, or opinion whatsoever
- Oddly formal or stilted tone ("I am very satisfied with this purchase")
- Uniform sentence length and structure throughout
- No concrete use case or personal detail
- Vague superlatives only ("best ever", "amazing quality")
- Suspiciously short (under 10 words) or long (multi-paragraph for simple product)
- Length disproportionate to product complexity
- Reads like answering a prompt rather than sharing experience

Human disingenuous signals (HD):
- Describes wrong product or features item does not have
- Extreme rating with zero specifics
- Review is about shipping or seller, not product
- Star rating contradicts review text
- Incentivised disclosure present ("received for honest review")
- Copy-pasted spec sheet language
- Emotional but completely vague ("life changing!!! love it!!!")
- No concrete use case or personal detail
- Vague superlatives only ("best ever", "amazing quality")
- Suspiciously short (under 10 words) or long (multi-paragraph for simple product)
- Length disproportionate to product complexity
- Reads like answering a prompt rather than sharing experience

Process:
1. Identify which signals are present. List them briefly.
2. Determine whether signals cluster toward CG, HD, or neither.
3. If no clear signals, classify as OR.
4. Output your label and a one-sentence justification referencing the strongest signals found.

Output format:
LABEL: [OR|CG|HD]
SIGNALS: [comma-separated list of signals detected]
JUSTIFICATION: [one sentence]
